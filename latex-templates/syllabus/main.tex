\section{Course Policies}
%----------------------------------------------------------------------

\subsection{Attendance}
Regular attendance is expected. Students who miss more than three classes without valid excuse may have their final grade reduced. Please notify the instructor in advance if you must miss a class.

\subsection{Late Work}
Late assignments will be penalized 10\% per day for up to three days. After three days, late work will not be accepted unless prior arrangements have been made. Extensions may be granted for documented emergencies.

\subsection{Academic Integrity}
Academic dishonesty, including cheating, plagiarism, and fabrication, will not be tolerated. All work submitted must be your own. Violations will be reported to the Office of Academic Integrity and may result in course failure. When in doubt about what constitutes academic dishonesty, please ask.

\subsection{Communication}
The best way to reach me is via email. I will respond within 24--48 hours on weekdays. For urgent matters, please indicate ``URGENT'' in your subject line. Check your university email and the course website regularly for announcements.

\subsection{Technology Policy}
Laptops and tablets are permitted for note-taking and class activities. Please silence all devices during class. Recording of lectures requires prior permission from the instructor.
