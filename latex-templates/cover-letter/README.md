# Cover Letter

Professional cover letter template for job or academic applications. Clean, formal format suitable for industry positions, academic jobs, and graduate school applications.

## Usage

1. Replace all `{{PLACEHOLDER}}` values with your information
2. Compile with pdfLaTeX: `pdflatex main.tex`
3. Customize the paragraph content to match your experience and the position

## Structure

The cover letter follows a standard business letter format:
- Header with your contact information
- Recipient's contact information
- Opening paragraph (express interest, mention position)
- Body paragraphs (highlight experience and skills)
- Closing paragraph (call to action)
- Formal closing and signature

## Requirements

- LaTeX distribution (TeX Live, MiKTeX, or MacTeX)
- Packages: `letter`, `geometry`, `hyperref`, `xcolor`

## Compilation

```bash
pdflatex main.tex
```

## Tips

1. Keep the letter to one page
2. Customize each letter for the specific position
3. Use concrete examples and metrics where possible
4. Proofread carefully before sending
5. Match the tone to the company culture
