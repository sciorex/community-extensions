\usepackage[margin=1in]{geometry}
\usepackage{hyperref}

    colorlinks=true,

    urlcolor=linkcolor,
    pdfauthor={{{YOUR_NAME}}},
    pdftitle={Cover Letter - {{POSITION}}}
}

% Remove default letter formatting
\longindentation=0pt

\signature{{{YOUR_NAME}}}

% Sender address
\address{
    {{YOUR_NAME}}\\
    {{YOUR_ADDRESS}}\\
    {{YOUR_CITY}}, {{YOUR_STATE}} {{YOUR_ZIP}}\\
    {{YOUR_EMAIL}}\\
    {{YOUR_PHONE}}
}

\begin{document}

\begin{letter}{
    {{RECIPIENT_NAME}}\\
    {{RECIPIENT_TITLE}}\\
    {{COMPANY_NAME}}\\
    {{COMPANY_ADDRESS}}\\
    {{COMPANY_CITY}}, {{COMPANY_STATE}} {{COMPANY_ZIP}}
}

\opening{Dear {{RECIPIENT_NAME}},}

% Opening paragraph - Express interest and mention the position
I am writing to express my strong interest in the {{POSITION}} position at {{COMPANY_NAME}}, as advertised on {{JOB_SOURCE}}. With my background in {{YOUR_FIELD}} and {{YEARS_EXPERIENCE}} years of experience in {{YOUR_EXPERTISE}}, I am confident that I would be a valuable addition to your team.

% Body paragraph 1 - Highlight relevant experience
In my current role as {{CURRENT_POSITION}} at {{CURRENT_COMPANY}}, I have successfully {{KEY_ACHIEVEMENT_1}}. This experience has equipped me with strong skills in {{SKILL_1}}, {{SKILL_2}}, and {{SKILL_3}}, which align directly with the requirements outlined in your job posting.

% Body paragraph 2 - Connect skills to company needs
I am particularly drawn to {{COMPANY_NAME}} because of {{COMPANY_ATTRACTION}}. Your commitment to {{COMPANY_VALUE}} resonates with my professional values, and I am excited about the opportunity to contribute to {{COMPANY_GOAL}}. My experience in {{RELEVANT_EXPERIENCE}} has prepared me to make immediate contributions to your team.

% Body paragraph 3 - Specific examples (optional, can be removed)
During my tenure at {{PREVIOUS_COMPANY}}, I {{KEY_ACHIEVEMENT_2}}. This accomplishment demonstrates my ability to {{DEMONSTRATED_SKILL}} and my commitment to delivering results that exceed expectations.

% Closing paragraph - Call to action
I would welcome the opportunity to discuss how my experience and skills can benefit {{COMPANY_NAME}}. I am available for an interview at your earliest convenience and can be reached at {{YOUR_PHONE}} or via email at {{YOUR_EMAIL}}. Thank you for considering my application. I look forward to hearing from you.

\closing{Sincerely,}
